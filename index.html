I am an AI & Data Science undergraduate at IIT Jodhpur and Founder of AYNX and SkillPraxis. My work focuses on building decision intelligence systems that operate at the intersection of machine learning, optimization theory, and quantum computing.

At AYNX, I am developing Optivara, a constraint-based optimization platform for enterprise environments. At SkillPraxis, I am reimagining technical education through hands-on capstone projects addressing real-world Indian industry challenges.

My approach is rooted in first-principles thinking—understanding how mathematics, algorithms, and computation interact to solve complex problems. I value depth, rigor, and clarity over shortcuts and surface-level learning.
